Features

Everything your remote team needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Woven combines async updates, project management, docs, and video into one calm, focused workspace.

Async Standup

Team members post short video or text updates on their own schedule. Woven compiles them into a digest that takes 3 minutes to skim — not 45 to watch.

Smart Projects

Kanban, list, or timeline — your choice. AI automatically creates tasks from meeting notes, assigns them based on team workload, and surfaces blockers before they become problems.

Meeting Intelligence

Record any call and Woven's AI writes the summary, extracts decisions, and creates follow-up tasks automatically. Your meetings earn their place on the calendar.

Team Visibility

See what everyone's working on without micromanaging. Real-time workload view lets managers spot over-allocation and under-utilisation across every time zone.

Connected Wiki

Docs that actually stay up to date. Pages auto-link to related tasks and decisions. Ask Woven AI any question about your company and it finds the answer from your own knowledge base.

250+ Integrations

Slack, GitHub, Figma, Salesforce, Linear — Woven pulls in context from wherever your work lives. One feed. Zero tab-switching.
