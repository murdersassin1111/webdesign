Our Approach

We design for
the 200-year view.

Most buildings are designed to last 30 years. We design buildings for 200. That changes every decision — from foundation depth to material sourcing to how the building will be decommissioned when its time comes.

01

Site-first design

We spend months understanding sun angles, prevailing winds, soil composition, and local ecology before a single line is drawn. The site tells us what to build.

02

Material honesty

We use mass timber, rammed earth, recycled steel, and locally sourced stone. We track embodied carbon for every project and publish the numbers openly.

03

Performance guaranteed

Every Terroir project comes with a 10-year energy performance guarantee. If it doesn't perform as modelled, we return the difference.

68%

Lower embodied carbon

Average vs conventional construction — independently verified by Carbon Leadership Forum

100%

Renewable energy design

Every project we design is capable of net-zero or net-positive energy operation through passive design and on-site generation
