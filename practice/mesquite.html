FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our CNC manufacturing services.

What CNC machining tolerances can Mesquite Manufacturing achieve? +

Mesquite Manufacturing achieves tolerances ranging from ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ using advanced fourth-axis CNC technology — suitable for the most demanding aerospace, medical, and industrial applications.

What materials does Mesquite Manufacturing work with? +

We work with a wide range of materials including aluminium, steel, stainless steel, titanium, brass, copper (metals), as well as ABS plastic, nylon, PEEK, Delrin (plastics), and carbon fibre and G10 composite materials.

Does Mesquite Manufacturing offer rapid prototyping? +

Yes. Our rapid prototyping service swiftly brings your concepts to life with an iterative feedback process. We identify and resolve design issues early — saving time and cost before committing to full production runs.

What is fourth-axis CNC machining and why does it matter? +

Fourth-axis CNC machining adds a rotational axis to standard 3-axis CNC, enabling complex geometries and intricate designs in a single setup. This means faster turnaround and lower cost on high-volume production runs compared to competitors using traditional 3-axis machines.
